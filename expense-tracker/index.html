This Privacy Policy describes how Expense Tracker ("we," "our," or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Android mobile application.

Introduction

Welcome to Expense Tracker ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using Expense Tracker, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

Our app stores all data locally on your device. We collect and process the following types of information:

  • Financial Data: Transaction records, expense amounts, income amounts, categories, notes, and recurring transactions
  • Budget Information: Budget limits, spending patterns, and budget categories
  • Authentication Data: PIN codes and biometric authentication data (fingerprint/face unlock - stored locally only using secure hardware)
  • App Settings: Theme preferences, language settings, notification preferences, and currency settings
  • Calendar Data: Transaction dates and budget periods for visualization and tracking
  • Analytics Data: Financial summaries and charts are generated locally on your device using your expense records.

Device Information

The app may access limited device information such as Android version and device model only to ensure compatibility. This information is processed locally and is not transmitted to our servers.

🔒 Important Note on Data Storage

All your financial data is stored locally on your device only. We do not upload your transaction data, expenses, income, or personal financial information to any remote servers. Your data remains completely private and under your control.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To provide core features like expense tracking, budget management, financial reports, charts, and calendar-based expense visualization
  • Security: To authenticate users through PIN or biometric authentication (fingerprint/face unlock) using device secure storage
  • User Experience: To personalize your experience with theme and language preferences
  • Data Backup & Export: To enable you to export your financial data in CSV format and share reports using your device's sharing functionality
  • Data Import: To allow you to import previously exported CSV files to restore your financial data
  • Financial Analytics: To generate charts, spending patterns, and budget insights using locally stored data
  • App Improvement: To analyze app performance and fix bugs (anonymous diagnostic data only, no personal financial data)

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All your personal and financial data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage mechanisms. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your sensitive information.

Security Measures

  • PIN Protection: Optional PIN lock to secure app access
  • Biometric Authentication: Fingerprint and face unlock support using your device's secure hardware
  • Encrypted Storage: Sensitive data is encrypted using Flutter Secure Storage and device security features (Hive database with encryption)
  • No Cloud Storage: Your financial data never leaves your device - no remote servers or cloud backup
  • Secure File Operations: CSV imports and exports use secure file handling to prevent data leakage

⚠️ Important: While we implement security measures, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security. Please use device security features like screen lock to protect your data.

Third-Party Services

Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may process certain device information required to serve ads in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy .

Expense Tracker does not share your financial records, transactions, budgets, or notes with AdMob.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can access all your data stored in the app at any time through the app interface
  • Export: You can export your complete financial data in CSV format for backup, analysis, or migration purposes
  • Import: You can import previously exported CSV files to restore your data
  • Delete: You can delete individual transactions, categories, or all your data from the app settings
  • Control: You can enable/disable security features like PIN and biometric lock at any time
  • Share: You can share expense reports through your device's sharing options while maintaining control over what you share
  • Ad Preferences: You can opt-out of personalized ads through your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization)

Data Retention

Your data is retained locally on your device indefinitely until you choose to delete it. We do not have access to your data, and it is not stored on our servers. When you uninstall the app, all local data is removed from your device.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Internet: To display AdMob ads
  • Biometric Permission: Optional fingerprint/face unlock for app security
  • Notifications: Optional reminders for budget alerts
  • File Access: The app only accesses files when you explicitly choose to import or export your data. We do not scan, collect, or upload files from your device

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

International Users

Our app can be used worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device, your information is subject to the laws and regulations of your country of residence.
